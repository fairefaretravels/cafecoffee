## Screener criteria (internal use — do not share)

**Include:**
- Q1: Beekeeping checked *(prioritize)*, or any farm type
- Q3: 5–200 hives *(sweet spot; fewer = hobby, more = enterprise)*
- Q4: Sells products (any channel)
- Q6: Paper or spreadsheets *(no dedicated app = ideal; shows unmet need)*
- Q7: Frustrated or "does the job but has gaps"
- Q10: Comfortable or somewhat comfortable *(avoid extremes on both ends)*

**Exclude:**
- Q1: None of the listed farm types
- Q3: Fewer than 5 hives AND hobby only
- Q4: No sales at all
- Q7: Very satisfied with current system *(low motivation to switch)*
- Q10: Not very comfortable *(usability signal gets muddied)*

**Aim for:**
- 3–4 beekeepers (core persona)
- 1–2 other farm types (generalizability check)
- Mix of spreadsheet and paper users
- At least one wholesale seller
